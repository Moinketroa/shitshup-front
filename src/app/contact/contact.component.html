Jean-Marc DEBICKI About Shitshup UI and me...

Thanks for using Shitshup UI !

This app is designed to facilitate multiple track processing actions. Instead of downloading, analyzing, seperating the stems and regrouping everything on a basic spreadsheet, Shitshup allows you to do everything at once.

Why Shitshup UI ?

After two MEGAMASHUPS (available on my YouTube Channel), I was concerned about having the latest meme musics in order to stay in touch with the viewers. But as the trends come and goes, I found that repeating the basic tasks of downloading, analyzing, spleeting the tracks,... were becomming to feel overwhelming. So I created this app in order to facilitate and automate the different tasks.
